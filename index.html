About Hackathon

Seshadripuram Educational Trust (SET) is a renowned organization dedicated to providing quality education. Seshadripuram First Grade College (SFGC), under SET, is a premier institution offering programs in Software Engineering Technology and other disciplines. Hackathon 2024 is a 24-hour innovation marathon organized by the Computer Department at SFGC, where tech enthusiasts come together to build groundbreaking solutions. Join us at SFGC to collaborate, code, and create the future of technology.
