SET

Seshadripuram Educational Trust (SET) is a renowned organization dedicated to providing quality education since 1930. The trust manages a vast network of institutions that foster academic excellence and character building across Karnataka.

SFGC

Seshadripuram First Grade College (SFGC), Yelahanka, is a premier institution under SET. It is committed to providing contemporary education in Software Engineering, Technology, and Management, preparing students for the global industry.

Hackathon

Hackathon 2026 is a high-energy, 24-hour innovation marathon hosted by the Computer Science Department. It brings together the brightest minds to collaborate, code, and create groundbreaking solutions for real-world problems.
